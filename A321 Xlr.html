Overview

The A321 XLR offers unmatched range and fuel efficiency in the single-aisle market, providing airlines with a versatile aircraft capable of flying long-haul routes. It features a spacious cabin with state-of-the-art amenities for passenger comfort.

  • Range: 8,700 km / 4,700 nmi
  • Seating Capacity: 180-220 passengers
  • Fuel Efficiency: 30% less fuel burn per seat

Technical Specifications

  • Length: 44.51 m / 146 ft
  • Wingspan: 35.80 m / 117 ft
  • Max Takeoff Weight: 97,000 kg / 213,848 lb
  • Engines: CFM LEAP-1A / PW1100G
  • Max Fuel Capacity: 32,940 L / 8,702 US gal

A321 XLR in Action

Engine

The A321 XLR is powered by advanced engines, offering reduced emissions and noise levels. The options include the CFM LEAP-1A and Pratt & Whitney PW1100G engines, both providing exceptional fuel efficiency and performance.
With both engines it provides a total of 70,000lbs which is equivalent to 93,800 Horse Powers!
