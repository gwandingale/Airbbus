A330 MRTT

The A330 MRTT (Multi Role Tanker Transport) is the most capable new generation tanker, combat-proven, and with unique multi-role capabilities. It provides strategic air transport, aerial refueling, and medical evacuation capabilities.

  • Payload: 111 tonnes / 244,000 lbs
  • Max Fuel Capacity: 111,000 kg / 245,000 lbs
  • Range: 14,800 km / 8,000 nmi

A400M

The A400M is the most versatile airlifter currently available, combining the capability to transport strategic loads, with the ability to deliver even into tactical locations. It can carry the heaviest loads in the most severe conditions.

  • Payload: 37 tonnes / 81,600 lbs
  • Range: 4,800 km / 2,600 nmi (with max payload)
  • Top Speed: 780 km/h / 485 mph
