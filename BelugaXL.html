Overview

The Airbus Beluga XL is a highly efficient and uniquely designed transport aircraft, specially developed to transport oversized cargo. With its impressive cargo capacity and advanced technology, it plays a crucial role in Airbus’s production and delivery operations.

  • Cargo Capacity: 53,000 kg / 116,000 lb
  • Payload Volume: 1,400 m³ / 49,500 ft³
  • Range: 4,000 km / 2,485 nmi

Technical Specifications

  • Length: 63.1 m / 207 ft
  • Wingspan: 60.3 m / 198 ft
  • Height: 19.8 m / 65 ft
  • Max Takeoff Weight: 227,000 kg / 500,000 lb
  • Engines: Rolls-Royce Trent 700
  • Max Cargo Volume: 1,400 m³ / 49,500 ft³

Beluga XL in Action

Engine

The Beluga XL is powered by the Rolls-Royce Trent 700 engines, known for their efficiency and reliability. These engines ensure optimal performance and low emissions, making the Beluga XL an environmentally friendly choice for oversized cargo transport.
